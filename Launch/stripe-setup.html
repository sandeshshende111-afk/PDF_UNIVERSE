💳 Payment Setup

Get Paid in 10 Minutes

Set up Stripe payments for PDFUniverse — no coding needed. By the end of this guide, your Pro plan will accept real credit card payments.

⏱ Takes exactly 10 minutes · Free to start
1
Create a free Stripe account
Stripe is the most trusted payment processor in the world. Used by Amazon, Shopify, and millions of businesses. Zero monthly fees — they take 2.9% + $0.30 per successful charge only.
  1. Go to stripe.com and click "Start now"
  2. Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email
  3. Fill in your business details (use your real name/address — this is for tax purposes)
  4. Add your bank account so payments can be deposited to you
  5. You'll land on the Stripe Dashboard — keep this open
Open Stripe Signup →
💡
Test mode vs Live mode: Stripe starts you in "Test Mode." You can test payments with fake cards before going live. Toggle to "Live Mode" in the top-left when you're ready to accept real money.
2
Create your products and prices
Tell Stripe what you're selling and how much. You need to create 4 products: Pro Monthly, Pro Annual, Team Monthly, Team Annual.
  1. In Stripe Dashboard, click Products in the left sidebar
  2. Click "Add product" (top right button)
  3. Create the 4 products below — one at a time:

How to create each: Click "Add product" → enter name → under Pricing, select "Recurring" → enter price → select billing period (Monthly or Yearly) → click Save. Repeat for all 4.
3
Create Payment Links (no coding!)
Stripe Payment Links are magic — a URL you share that opens a secure payment page. No website integration needed. Click the link → user pays → you get the money.
  1. In Stripe Dashboard → click "Payment Links" in the sidebar
  2. Click "New payment link"
  3. Select your product (e.g., "PDFUniverse Pro") → click Continue
  4. Customize the page (add your logo if you have one)
  5. Under "After payment" → set Redirect URL to your site: https://your-site.netlify.app/
  6. Click "Create link"
  7. Copy the link — it looks like: https://buy.stripe.com/xxxxxxxxxxxxx
  8. Repeat for all 4 products
✓
Your payment links are now live! Anyone who clicks them can pay with a credit card. Stripe handles everything — security, receipts, subscriptions, cancellations.

5
Test your first payment
Before going live, test the entire payment flow with Stripe's test card numbers. No real money is charged during testing.
  1. Make sure Stripe is in Test Mode (toggle in top-left of dashboard)
  2. Click your payment link → it opens the Stripe checkout page
  3. Use test card number: 4242 4242 4242 4242
  4. Use any future expiry date (e.g., 12/34) and any 3-digit CVC (e.g., 123)
  5. Complete the payment — you should see a success page
  6. Check Stripe Dashboard → Payments — the test payment should appear
  7. When happy, switch Stripe to Live Mode and create new live payment links

+
Bonus: Know exactly who paid (Zapier method)
When someone pays, you want to know immediately and send them a welcome email. Without a backend, use Zapier to connect Stripe to your email service — completely free, no coding.
  1. Go to zapier.com → sign up free
  2. Click "Create Zap"
  3. Trigger: Search "Stripe" → select "New Payment" → connect your Stripe account
  4. Action: Search "Gmail" (or Mailchimp) → select "Send Email"
  5. Set up the email: To = customer email, Subject = "Payment confirmed!", Body = your welcome message
  6. Test it → turn it on
⚡
Zapier's free tier allows 100 automated actions/month — enough for 100 new paying customers. When you exceed that, upgrade to Zapier Starter ($20/mo) or set up proper email automation with Resend.
