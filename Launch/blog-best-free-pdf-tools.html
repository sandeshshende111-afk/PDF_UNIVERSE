  • Quality — does the output match the input in fidelity?

FAQ

Is iLovePDF still free in 2025?

Yes, but with limits — task limits, ads, and a 100MB file cap. The free tier still works for occasional use. If you need more, iLovePDF Premium is $4/month or PDFUniverse Pro is $12/month with additional AI features.

Is it safe to upload PDFs to online tools?

It depends on the tool. Reputable tools like iLovePDF and Sejda delete files after 1–2 hours. PDFUniverse never uploads files at all — processing happens in your browser. For truly confidential documents (medical, legal, financial), use PDFUniverse or LibreOffice.

What's the best free alternative to Adobe Acrobat?

For online use: PDFUniverse for privacy and features, or PDF24 for unlimited free tasks. For offline editing: LibreOffice Draw is a genuinely capable free alternative. None of them match the full Adobe Acrobat desktop app for complex editing, but for 95% of users' needs, these free tools are sufficient.
