Privacy Policy

Last Updated January 1, 2025 · Effective Immediately

Short version: PDFUniverse processes PDF files locally in your browser. We never read, store, or share your documents. Files never touch our servers during basic operations. We collect only the minimum data needed to run the service.

PDFUniverse ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using PDFUniverse, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address.
  • Payment information: If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We never store your full credit card number — only the last 4 digits and expiry date for display purposes.
  • Communications: If you contact us by email, we retain those communications to respond and improve support.

1.2 Files You Process

For browser-based tools (most tools): Your PDF files are processed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. The files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers. We cannot access, read, or store these files.

For server-based tools (Pro AI features only): When you use AI Summarization or OCR features, the document text (not the file itself) is sent to our AI processing partner (Anthropic) to generate a response. This text is not stored beyond the processing of your request.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data: We collect anonymized information about which tools are used and how often, to improve the service. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution — used to ensure the site works correctly across devices.
  • IP address: Logged by our hosting provider (Netlify) for security and abuse prevention. Retained for 30 days.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for authentication (keeping you logged in). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the PDFUniverse service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, password resets, service notices)
  • To send product updates and newsletters (only if you opted in; unsubscribe any time)
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • To comply with legal obligations

We never sell your personal data to any third party. We never use your data for advertising targeting.

3. Data Retention

  • Browser-processed files: Never retained (processed locally, never sent to us)
  • Server-processed files (Pro AI features): Automatically deleted within 1 hour of processing
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • Server logs (IP addresses): Retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted

4. Third Parties We Work With

We share data with the following categories of third parties only to operate our service:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — AI processing for Pro features. Text content sent for AI analysis. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify — website hosting. IP addresses and access logs per their privacy policy.
  • Resend / Mailchimp — email delivery. Email addresses shared to send you emails you've requested.

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties not listed above.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • AES-256 encryption for any data stored at rest
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates
  • Minimal data collection (we don't store what we don't need)
  • Strict access controls — only authorized team members can access user data

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@pdfuniverse.app.

7. Children's Privacy

PDFUniverse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us at privacy@pdfuniverse.app and we will delete it immediately.
